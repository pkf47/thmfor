About

Volta is a clean and modern Photography Wordpress Theme that is perfect to promote your photo gallery in a very professional and pleasant way. Volta is made of HTML5 and CSS3 using best wordpress framework Redux. It is fully responsive; it looks stunning on all types of screens and devices (desktop, notebook, tablet and mobile).

Download theme

Volta comes in three variations: - Image Background - Video Background - Content Slider

If you have questions about anything in Volta template, please send us an e-mail. Have fun with your new template and thanks again for buying! Note: some features will not work until you upload your file on your sever.

Fullscreen background video Fullscreen background slider Fullscreen background images Fully responsive Albums Portfolio that supports Image, Video, Slider, Vimeo, Youtube and SoundCloud Easy to customize Documentation included Valid HTML5 / CSS3 CSS3 Content Animations jQuery Enhanced Cross-browser Compatible (IE 11, Safari, Chrome, Opera, Firefox)
