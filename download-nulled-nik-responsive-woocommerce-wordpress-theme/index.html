Nik – Absolutely Stunning WordPress Theme for Men’s Fashion store

Nik is a simple yet elegant, multipurpose theme. Following the new trends in design, this is an eye-catching and straightforward theme with flat design, 5 color presets, neat typography, smooth navigation and well-structured layout. Nik would be a perfect choice for any eCommerce websites, especially men’s fashion, jewelry or wedding website…

Download theme

Furthermore, this amazing theme is integrated with , many plugins with lots of features, mini cart, custom widgets, unlimited color schemes, Slider Resolution with smooth transition effects, Mega Menu with multiple column styles and advanced widgets… You are free to control this theme in order to make your store more friendly and adorable for your customers..

Let’s experience this awesomeness and to explore more features in this theme. We are sure that you will fall in love with our Nik at first sight.

UNLIMITED COLOR STYLES

This theme comes with a lots of color styles: Tomato, Organe, Cyan, Boocdo and Blue. Besides, it gives you the full control in customizing color schemes in back-end.

WIDE AND BOXED LAYOUTS

Nik supports boxed and wide layout, with boxed version you can easily customize the background image and change settings to meet your idea.

RESPONSIVE DESIGN

Nik is designed to be fully responsive layout, it will make your site have amazing look on different screen resolutions such as: mobile, iPhone, iPad and other tablets. This is one of the best option to reach the customers who use smart devices.

MENU STYLES

You can easy to create columns to display categories and products with description, price, image, video, effects… or even add links to any page, widgets with promotion banners and plugins.

STUNNING SLIDESHOW

This theme promise that it will attract the highest viewer’s attention when surfing your site by using Revolution Slider. Supporting tons of transition effects, an image preloader, video embedding, autoplay that stops on user interaction and lots of easy to set options to create your own effects just from the admin.

Create image slideshow widget instance and display on any position Create unlimited layered per slide, configure various effects. Configure slideshow effect, auto sliding, thumbnail, text, navigation… WOOCOMMERCE INTEGRATION

Theme uses the excellent WooCommerce plugin to transform your WordPress website into an eCommerce store. Plugin supports to show a single product/multiple products by ID or SKU and a lot of payment options and shipping options available.

DISPLAY NEW PRODUCTS, BEST SELLER PRODUCTS

This feature is to keep the website being fresh and easy structured for visitors following. The product listing is shown as a dynamic slider.

ADD TO WISHLISH – ADD TO COMPARE

This theme extends the shop functionality of WooCommerce plugin with additional features: Product Wishlist and Product Comparison functionalities.

QUICK VIEW PRODUCT

It’s so convenient for customers to view quickly detail product on the Homepage, Listing page without leaving the current page. Your product detail will open with full necessary information like: image product, product price, description, product reviews and link to the product detail page, etc.

AJAX MINICART

Be built-in Minicart widget, you can select product to buy without leaving current page, instantly add product to basket. We also re-style of the Checkout page to increase user experience and sales. Additional, you can easy to add product to wishlish or compare product.

ALTERNATIVE IMAGE ON MOUSE HOVER

To give customers the best shopping experience, Nik is equipped with a wonderful hover action. An alternative product’s image will be displayed when users move the mouse over.

TRANSLATION SUPPORT & RTL LANGUAGE READY

This theme is fully compatible with the WPML multilingual plugin, you can translate your website to any language. SW Nik comes in two languages versions: LTR and RTL to respond to users who communicate with a right-to-left language. Theme includes setting the direction: RTL CSS style and adjusting widget layout and behavior accordingly, and RTL will be activated when you set up RTL language in WP-Config.

CATEGORY PAGE

By selecting Category, your customer can find exactly product they want faster. This helps enhance the customer’s shopping experience.

GRID AND LIST VIEW

The 2 styles of grid and list display help your customers see the products fast and easily. Customer can flexibly change the display styles to view product as list or grid.

PRODUCT PAGE

Your customers can easily find deeper information about a product before making a decision to order

MULTIPLE BLOG STYLES

Nik support various nice and clean Blog style: Blog with Left Sidebar, Blog with Right Sidebar, Blog page with 2,3,4,5 and 6 columns. With SW Nik, creating a blog for your store are much easier than ever.

USEFUL SHORTCODES

Theme includes tons of useful built-in Shortcodes such as: Columns, Slideshow, Button, Google Map… You can create advanced custom content with minimal effort or even without any knowledge how to code HTML. Shortcodes can be used anywhere in pages, posts and also in menu.
