The Law Practice is a clean and stark looking lawyer WordPress theme specifically designed and developed to meet the needs of ** advocate, attorneys, barrister, counsel ,business, consultancy, finance, justice, lawyer, legal adviser, legal office, responsive, solicitor,accountant,law ** and many others that need a corporate website. The theme is built for Legal Advisers, Legal offices, Lawyers, Attorneys, Barristers at Law, Counsels, Solicitors, Advocates **and other legal and law related services and it is suitable for any kind of legal and financial activity – **Barristers at Law, Counsels, Solicitors, Advocates, Accountant, Consultant, Consulting, Counsellor. It includes all the necessary functionality to present legal services online. Custom Services post type lets you provide a gallery of team in an attractive and handy way, describing your cases and practices in a catchy way. If there is a team of lawyers working for your office, Lawyer theme provides a great way to showcase attorney personal profiles.
