The Breaking News is a minimalist WordPress theme with a modern and fresh design. Also, theme has lots of white space and easy-to-read typography. The Breaking News theme is ready for mobile devices. The Breaking News theme features a featured slideshow and editor’s choice section for showcasing your most important articles. This theme is perfect for news and magazine sites as well as blogs.
