About Photographer

Photographer is a responsive WordPress theme created for creative Photographers with a minimalist design and optimized for mobile.

Features

Minimalistic & Distraction Free Design Optimal Image Viewing Experience On Any Device with zoomable images Live Photowall Homepage – in every visit you see a random unique homepage generated from your portfolio images randomly. Responsive Design & Retina ready Easy to use; create galleries with wordpress built in galleries HTML5, CSS3, jQuery powered Flexible Images & Videos 2 blog layouts (with or without sidebar versions) Complete blog system (all post formats supported) Unlimited Portfolios (Muliple portfolio page support) Responsive video embed in various aspect ratios (youtube/vimeo …) 30+ social media icons Unlimited Sidebars Custom Widgets (Flickr, Instagram, Pinterest, Dribbble, Picasa, YouTube) Custom Settings Panel Theme Customizer Support 600+ Google Fonts Ajax contact form with validation SEO & Google Analytics ready Custom Shortcodes Translation Ready (.po files included) Extensive Documentation &
