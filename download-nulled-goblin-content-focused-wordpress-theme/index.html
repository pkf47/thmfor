Download nulled Goblin - Content-Focused WordPress Theme


Goblin is a minimalist and content-focused WordPress Theme. It was designed for readability on all devices. The theme supports post formats and can display images, videos and audio at full width. It can be used as a personal blog, portfolio, journal and it’s very easy to setup and use.

Unlimited Header Styles Option

With 5 header templates: Standard, Full Screen, Center Logo, Mini and Off Canvas Header. One click to change, you can also setting header color scheme, sticky, include social box, display style and change header direction.

Mix Your Own Style

Easy to make your site Unique just a few clicks with: Boxed/Wide Layout + 5 Header Style + 4 Intro Style + 2 Site Modes + 2 Post List Style + 3 Footer Styles and Unlimited Colors.

Create Your Own Stories

8 Custom Post Formats, you can easy change your idea in each post with: Display Style, Cover Image, Background Color, Mask Layer Options, Waveform Options …

Great parallax scrolling

Advanced Typography

One click to change the Font (600+), Colors (22+) and Title Format.

Advanced Theme Option

Goblin built-in Advanced Theme Option that allow you easy to control your site. Just a few clicks.
