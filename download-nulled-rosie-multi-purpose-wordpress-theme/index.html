Rosie is responsive WordPress Theme suited for creative designers, photographers, bloggers, product or service landing pages and even corporate web sites. Easy to setup and it comes with wide color customization, extended typography settings, Typekit support and fully customizable sections.

Download theme

Rosie – Multi-Purpose Theme includes the innovative and state-of-art techniques and design features such as trasparent menus and elements, parallax effects, motion backgrounds, full page scroll etc.

Advanced Font Editor allows to select theme fonts from a preset of standard web fonts, google fonts, or custom uploadable fonts. Import/Export for Site Content allows to handle importing and exporting of the site content between installations including posts, menus etc. Easy DEMO site installation includes all necessary data, sliders, pictures and videos.

We care about simplicity and usability, we are users just like you. Rosie comes with intuitive, easy to use AJAX based administration interface. Lifetime free updates and support of course. Please ask for assistance or just say hello. We would love to hear from you!
