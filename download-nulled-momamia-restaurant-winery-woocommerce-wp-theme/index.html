Download nulled Momamia - Restaurant & Winery WooCommerce WP Theme


Momamia is a mediterranean style Wordpress theme perfect for restaurants, wineries, wine tour companies, cafes and businesses looking for a relaxing outdoors feel. Built with Greek, french and Italian restaurants in mind. Would also suit a B&B in the country, or a romantic tour, wedding venue, winery. It has been carefully designed, coded and tested.

Download theme

Features:

- Fade or Slide transitions for homepage slider. - 11 beautiful slide backgrounds and a foreground image template psd. - Responsive, tested on Android Phones, iPhones, iPads, OSX and Windows Desktops. - Homepage Sliders with a simple settings panel. No over complication. - Dinner/Wine Menu system. Add titles, subtitles, basic/multi price and wine items. - Social Links in menu, many popular social networks and website icons included. - International Language Support, Edit commonly used words directly from the Theme Options. - Customizable Social share buttons. - Drop down mini-cart, updated instantly with AJAX - Open Times widget and shortcode. - Well organized Theme Options, 90+ options to customize. - Google Fonts. - Bootstrap Shortags - with BS Shortcodes plus additional Unf Shortags. - Homepage Recent News Ticker - WooCommerce Integration - Carefully restyled without touching core files. - Really Nice Recent post widgets. - Google Maps Anywhere! - 4 Footer Sidebars. - 5 Page Sidebars that fall back to the blog sidebar if left blank. - 1 Level Bootstrap 3.1 Dropdown menus (mobile friendly) - Additional Author profile fields for Post author profiles. - Google Analytics Ready. - Responsive video embeds. - Shortcodes in Text Widgets. - SVG uploads.
