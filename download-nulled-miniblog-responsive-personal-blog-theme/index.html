2 Sliders

MiniBlog has 2 slider positions. One slider is for any kind of information and other slider is for showing featured posts.

Custom Background Option for Posts

You can choose different background color and image for each and every post. You can also set opacity for background color. Giving a unique look to your blog.

16 Layout Options

MiniBlog gives you the option to choose from 16 different layouts. You can check the demo of all the layouts in theme demo under Home menu. You can give your blog a different look using any one of the layout along with custom background and color option.

Social Media Share Buttons

MiniBlog gives you the option to show social media sharing buttons below articles. So you don’t need to install third party plugins.
