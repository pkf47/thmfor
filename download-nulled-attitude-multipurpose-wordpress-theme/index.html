Attitude is the difference between a good and a bad Theme design, where design is not just shapes and colors, but user experience and interface usability! Modern color scheme packed with clean layouts and typography is going to be a solid base for any type of content or portfolio based website.

Features

Newbie-Proof Enabled: Powerfull Drag & Drop Page Builder Preview Options before Applying them: Native WordPress Live Customizer For your Worldwide Audience: WPML Fully Compatible Made for Humans: Clean Typography and Layouts Business Minded: Truly Multipurpose Beast Choose Your Front Page: Different Slider Types Modular (On/Off) Widgetized Footer: Prefooter, Footer & Postfooter Modules Present Your Case Studies: Full Portfolio System Share Your Stories: Blog Post Layouts Say again, What do you Offer: Services Page Multipurpose at it’s Best: Shortcodes & Custom Widgets In Contact with Customers: Working Contact forms In the Known: Social Media implementation Display Aware: Responsive & Retina Ready Best Practices: Clean & Higly Customizable Code Get Your Clients to Approve the Project: No “I’m Stuck” Moments: Friendly, One on One Support!
