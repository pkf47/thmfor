BOKEH WordPress Theme Quick Overview

Bokeh is an elegant and clean WordPress theme with a onepage version. It’s a great choice for your next website project. The onepage version is powered by Ajax which loads inner pages smoothly with a 3d rotating navigation menu.

With its glassy look in both dark and light versions, it’s suitable for any blog, portfolio and photography WordPress websites. Also the light version looks great as a business website.
