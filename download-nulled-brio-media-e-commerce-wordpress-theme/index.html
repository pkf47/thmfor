Download nulled Brio - Media & E-Commerce WordPress Theme


Brio is a responsive theme with a flexible flat minimal design that works as a personal blog and allows you to setup an online store thanks to its integration with the WooCommerce plugin.

Download theme

Customize the post’s background and font colors on a post by post basis Full support of Google Web Fonts with the option to select the character subset for your language. Customize the background color and image, as well as the typography of every area in the site. Select a general layout for the site and a dedicated layout for single posts view. Add fullscreen backgrounds for single posts Color and font social icons to make it easy for your visitors to stay connected. Header with custom navigation menu and widgets area. Flexible footer with 3 widget areas. Fully compatible with WooCommerce plugin to easily create your online e-commerce shop. Custom layout for the Shop main page Shop with masonry layout Customized collapsible Shopping Cart on header area. Custom footer texts to add your copyright notice or anything else. Add your own logo image and hide or show your site title and tagline. Header slider showing images, videos and posts. Widget areas at the top and at the bottom. Welcome Message that can be shown at the top or at the bottom, with optional call to action button. In Business template mode, add highlights and/or testimonials.
