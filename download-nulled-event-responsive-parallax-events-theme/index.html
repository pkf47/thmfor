Event is a theme that is bold, fluid, and responsive. Built for music, event, and entertainment sites, Event comes with three custom post types specifically built for the event theme: Event, Gallery, and Video posts. Create complex page layouts with parallax scrolling and animation effects from scratch, in minutes, show casing your upcoming Events, and showing off photos and videos of past events. The perfect WordPress theme for bands, nightclubs, restaurants, bars, and more!

Event Post Type

Keep your visitors up to date on upcoming and past events using the Event post type found only in the Event theme from Themify. Take a look at our to see the different flavours of event posts, including the slider and various grid layouts. Event posts include an easy date and time picker for event date and time information, as well as options to include a map of the venue, location of the venue, and a link to buy tickets.

Gallery Post Type

Gallery post type includes a beautiful slider that automatically changes the background color of the container, based on the pixel colors from the image. Check out the ! Beside the slider layout, Galleries are available in 4-column, 3-column, and 2-column layouts, and our Masonry Gallery is fluid and responsive enough to allow you to display between 1 and 9 photos per row. Video Post Type

Event theme includes the ability to post Video posts, allowing you to embed videos from YouTube, Vimeo, and other video hosts, by posting the video URL. It automatically pulls the video embed thumbnail as the featured image for the post. You can also host your own custom videos and embed them using HTML5, and all videos can be displayed in a slider or various column layouts (view ), and they can be played in a responeive lightbox!

Statistics Icons & Share

All posts on Event come with their own post statistics icons and share buttons, showing off the number of post views, comment counts, and users who have liked the post. Share badges are also included, showing the number of shares, combined, from social media websites like Twitter, Facebook, Pinterest, and Google+. Sharing your events has made easier!
