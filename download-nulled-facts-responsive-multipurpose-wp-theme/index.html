FACTS is a minimalist, complete and flexible WP theme that brings you unlimited options to set up a Corporate, Creative or Photography business including smashing TD Menus. Create the most spectacular and unique site in any color, font or language! Make use of Facts exquisite and unique Sequence and Boxed image features.

Download theme

Facts home page layout options are truly limitless. FACTS is Responsive, Mobile ready, Multilingual, and ready for Visual Composer, the Layer Slider including the TD-shortcode plugin with over 200 short codes.

Make us of unlimited freedom in design, colors, and fonts. Create 1140 grid pages, full screen pages, or full width pages, and include transparent or colored (Parallax, Split or Boxed Image) sections. Design pages including header slider, background image, featured image, sidebars and footer sidebars.
