Download nulled SEK iThemes Exchange Shop WordPress Theme


SEK is an eCommerce theme for WordPress built on iThemes Exchange eCommerce platform, powered by Visual Composer and Revolution Slider. This simple but extendable platform helps you sell your digital and physical products extremely fast (can be configured in less than 5 minute) and has a simple interface. The free version of the Exchange plugin features cart, shipping, downloads, coupons and customers modules, but with the purchase of PRO Pack you get access to some other cool modules, like memberships, product variants, invoices and more. You have available 2 payment gateways already built-in Paypal and Stripe. Regarding the theme, the design is flat and simple with calm colors and custom modules specially created for easy content creation. The primary colors used are blue and grey specially chosen because of their visual influence on the user and their contribution to a better browsing and shopping experience. You can create unlimited page variations with the help of Visual Composer and Revolution Slider.
