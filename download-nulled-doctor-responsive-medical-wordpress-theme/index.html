Download nulled Doctor+: Responsive Medical WordPress Theme


Doctor+ is a successor of our previous Doctor WordPress Theme. We’ve taken everything that you liked and made it even better! Theme includes easy to use Page Builder and tons of options to customize the theme right from the admin panel. Whole admin panel and frontend parts are fully localized to 23 languages. Theme is multilingual right out of the box, you don’t need to purchase any additional plugin.
