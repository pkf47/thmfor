Sports & Life is a premium WordPress theme for gym and fitness websites. It is a highly suitable theme for sports clubs, gyms, fitness centers, and other exercise related websites. It has purpose oriented design, responsive layout and special features to manage classes, trainers, timetables, testimonials, news posts and pages.

Download theme

Easy & Extensive Theme Options

Sports & Life theme has a comprehensive set of theme options that will help you configure and modify the things around your website more easily.

Responsive Design

Sports & Life theme is built in twitter bootstrap, So it has a fully responsive layout. Now, your site will look awesome on big screens as well as on tablets and smart phones

Search Engine Optimized

This theme is fully hand coded and its HTML is written keeping in mind your sites good ranking on search engines. The theme has good use of h1,h2,h3 tags and most of its contents supports basic micro-formats.

Translation Ready

Sports & Life theme is translation ready. You can translate your website in any language using given .po and .mo files.

Google Fonts Support

Sports & Life theme provides theme options to change the site fonts to any Google font.

Various Styling Options

Sports & Life theme provides easy to use style customization options with in theme options. So, you can change the backgrounds and colors for many portions of the site.

Child Theme Included

Sports & Life theme comes with full support for child themes and one sample child theme is included as part of the package.

Easy & Comprehensive Documentation

Easy to understand and detailed documentation is included as part of the theme package. It will help you set up the theme quickly and get your most questions answered right away.
