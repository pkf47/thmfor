Genie is a simple, personal blog theme with distinctive typography and clean layout. No matter which blog topic you are about to cover, you will find Genie’s features a perfect fit. It is fully Responsive, Retina ready and Easy to customize.

With Genie your personal blog or magazine can be online in minutes while being fully supported by our highly acclaimed team.
