Download nulled Travelista - Premium WordPress Blog Theme


Travelista is a blog theme designed and developed for travel bloggers. But its simple and clean design can be used for any kind of blog.

Download theme

Travelista supports 9 post formats (Standard, Gallery, Link, Quote, Video, Image, Status Audio and Aside). It also provides an easy option to choose 9 different layouts for blog and archive pages and 3 different layouts for single pages.

Custom Background Options for Posts and Pages:

Travelista provides you the option to choose different background for each and every post or page. Whether your blog has 10 posts or 10,000 posts, you can set different background for each and every post or page.

Custom Layout Options for Posts and Pages:

You have the option to choose different layouts for each and every post or page. You can choose left sidebar, right sidebar or full width layout for different posts.

Beautiful Footer with Multiple Columns:

Footer of travelista is beautifully designed with option to add logo with background. Travelista also has the option to choose among 4 columns, 3 columns, 2 columns or single column layout for footer widets.

Custom Color Scheme and Background Options for Category Pages

You can choose different color scheme or background for each and every category page.

Re-arrangeable Social Icons:

You can show social icons in header and footer easily from theme options panel. You can also rearrange the position of social icons.
