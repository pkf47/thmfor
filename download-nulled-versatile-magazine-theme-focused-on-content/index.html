Download nulled Versatile: Magazine Theme Focused on Content


Versatile is a quintessential WordPress theme for a serious, successful online publisher. Select from 3 different grid views (masonry, brick, and photogrid) and customize it via Theme Customizer to suit your need. With support of various post formats, create your own visual style.

Versatile features a responsive layout, from video to off-canvas sidebar, which means it’ll look great on a tablet or phone. So, no matter what screen your visitors are using, VER will fit perfectly.
