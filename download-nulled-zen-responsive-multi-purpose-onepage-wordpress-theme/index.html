Zen – Responsive Multi-Purpose OnePage WordPress Theme

ZEN is a perfect responsive Wordpress theme for those who desire simple but bold and contemporary look, with attention to details and typography. It is suitable for corporate, studio, agency, creative, or any general business.

Download theme

ZEN is responsive multi-purpose onepage theme with included out of box blog, it comes in 3 predefined colors, (green, blue and purple) and different background options including slideshow, parallax and video. We have included easy to follow instructions on how to setup and edit, in no time you’ll have your site up and running!
