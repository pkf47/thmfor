Download nulled Odyssey - Light Responsive WordPress Blog Theme


Light. Modern. Minimal. Powerful. Optimised. Smart. Responsive.

Download theme

“Odyssey Theme” combines minimal clean design & readability. It is responsive in every way it could be, emphatically.Although it has mostly basic functionality and intended for personal blog, focusing on every detail has resulted in mature solid theme with some real nice features and layout decisions for your next fancy blog. It’s also packed with Redux framework, which is filled up with handy options to give your site a personal look. Furthermore it comes with Visual Composer Plugin and it’s a $28 save for your wallet. It allows you to easily create and manage layouts of your pages and posts, it even has few shortcodes of our own. One of the main features of the theme is a carousel showcasing your posts in the header of the site. It could be displayed on any page. And it has some nice options to make it look unique. You can also display you latest posts in several ways: 1-3 columns masonry or simple list view.

Please remember to rate the theme, if you like it! Your feedback is very important to us.
