Download nulled Shutter - Photography & Art WordPress Theme


THIS IS SHUTTER, A THEME DEDICATED TO PHOTOGRAPHERS
Do you have the feeling that you’re getting tired of creating a lot of albums and uploads that mess up your entire site map, and make your visitors a hard time visiting your site?

Download theme

Shutter gives you back a simple and classic way of using your page templates as albums. Just add the images, add titles or specify them in categories and just publish. Just create new pages for new albums and add them on your menu. You’re able to filter your photographs inside your page too. Aperture makes it easy to handle a photography site with even small or large amount of photos.

TEMPLATES
Use “Gallery Slider” to introduce yourself or your company. Use “Gallery Horizontal” & “Gallery Horizontal Small” to present several photography thumbs at the same time and give your users the first impression for your portfolio. Use “Gallery Slider Small, Panorama Horizontal, Panorama Vertical” to show high quality and resolution images that need bigger preview to show off the details. Use “Gallery Circles or Gallery Mix” for special presentation pages.

WHAT IS MADE FOR?
It has everything you need to build a photographers website, several gallery templates to present your work with simple sliders and lightboxes, several about pages to present yourself or your company’s team. There is a contact form builder so you can build your “Get a quote form” and even a pricelist shortcode to show off your offers. The theme has also several portfolio templates to present your projcets, case studies or design artwork.

SIMPLICITY
The theme is mainly dedicated to present photography and artwork, so you won’t find a page builder with a million unnesessary stuff, but a simple theme panel with minimal options to costumize this theme for yourself or your clients.

COSTUMIZATION
There are two skins available, a white (default) and a dark skin. Change the branding color to adapt with your identity.

DEMO CONTENT
The .xml file is included in the package so you can import the database and have your site look exactly as our demo. just install the wordpress importer and have your site ready in minutes.

PSD FILES
Please contact us via contact form in our profile and we’ll send you the PSD files.
