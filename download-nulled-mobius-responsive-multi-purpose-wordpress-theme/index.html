Mobius is a multipurpose, clean, retina ready and fully responsive WordPress Theme. It is suited for any kind of website such as business and corporate sites, e-shops, agencies, creative portfolio, blog and photograph sites.
The theme is very flexible and it comes with some awesome and unique features, such as Themeone Slider, Mobius Grid, Custom Headers, Custom Sections, Parallax Sections, which make it perfect for showcasing and selling your work and products.
The Theme is built on top of the Redux Framework and offers tones of powerful and flexible settings/options. Presets for blog and portfolio layout was build in order to save your time.
