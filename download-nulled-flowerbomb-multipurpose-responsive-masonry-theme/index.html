FlowerBomb is a Modern Multipurpose Responsive Masonry WordPress Theme with a clean and professional design which will be a great solution for your business, portfolio, blog or any other purpose.

FlowerBomb is a modern template with fully responsive design. It has a wide range of features and comes with popular isotope plugin. It is very easy to customize and comes with detailed documentation and top notch support.

The template has six styles of homepage layout + other page templates you can use. With tons of layout possibilities, unlimited variations, multiple colours and a bunch of features you can create one unique variation in just a moment.
