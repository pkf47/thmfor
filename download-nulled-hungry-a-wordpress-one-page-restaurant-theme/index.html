Easy Recipes

Recipes in Hungry are super easy to manage thanks to WordPress’s custom post types. You can also assign your recipes to as many menus as you want.

Menu Shortcodes

Display your menus with ease with a simple shortcode. You can use the Shortcode on any page or post. If you are not keen on using Shortcodes, there is also an easy-to-use alternative in the theme options panel available by dragging and dropping menus in different columns to suit your needs.

Built-in Sections

All the sections you see in the demo are built into the theme and can be easily configured within the theme options panel. You can also add, re-order and remove these sections as you see fit by using a simple drag and drop interface. Custom sections are also supported too by using content from your other pages.

Included Custom Widgets

Contact Details – Display your key contact details such as your email address, phone number, and business address. Opening Times – Let your visitors know which days you are open and the best times to visit. Latest Recipes – Your visitors can see what’s new on your menu!

Theme Options

Hungry also comes with a powerful and robust theme options panel thanks to the Redux Framework. Configure the theme’s main elements such as the accent colour, one-page layout, right up to adding your own custom CSS and loads more!

Works with Open Table

Hungry also works well with the Open Table Widget so you can integrate your site with Open Table’s website. Simply use a shortcode within the Reservation section and voila! Please note that you will need to buy the PRO version of the widget to use its advanced features. Its available .
