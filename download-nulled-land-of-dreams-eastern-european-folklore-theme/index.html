Download nulled Land Of Dreams Eastern-European Folklore Theme


Download theme

Bright, decorated with Eastern-European folk art elements theme with blog and mini shop. Fine for people and organisations dealing with folk art, handcrafting, national musical and artistry heritage.

Overview

“Land of Dreams” is built on native WordPress functionality and has no shortcodes, page builders, third party Theme Options panels and plugins. All plugins (widgets, custom post types, metaboxes) were written by me to cater the concept of this theme. All plugins are very simple in use and do not consume time to understand how they works. Features

Blog and mini shop (based on PayPal minicart.js) Two types of sidebar (for all pages and for Shop) Separate colour scheme stylesheet for easier editing Post formats: Audio, Video, Gallery, Aside Audioplayer with up to 5 songs for Audio post format YouTube video or Featured image for standard post format Custom widgets: Video, Image, Google Adsense Metaboxes: Shop item information, HTML helper Body font suits Latin and Cyrillic languages Options via Customiser: logo, background, decorative elements Extended backend Suitable for multiple authors Page templates: Shop, About, Contact, Join Working contact form Responsive and suits various screen resolutions

Tests

OS: Windows, Mac, iPhone S4 Browsers: Safari, Chrome, Opera, Firefox, IE, Chrome Canary Validation: W3C validator, Theme Check, Test Unit

Credits

Fonts: Droid Serif, Petit Formal Script, EB Garamond, Websymbol Icon font Images: Wikimedia Commons, XIX century paintings, photographs and lithographs, (not included) Decorative: free patterns and elements Scripts: minicart.js (by PayPal), audio.js (by Anthony Kolber) Contact form: Frank Bueltge
