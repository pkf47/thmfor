InShape theme will be perfect for personal trainers, fitness aficionados and even sport gyms. We are confident that this theme will help everyone lose weight, trim the fat and get in shape!

Download theme

Our main goal with this niche theme was to provide the above mentioned professionals with fitness specific tools, that will help get the point across better. So, we have built a cool Workouts module that lets you easily add your programs by workout days, muscles worked, intensity, level, and lots more. Also, we have integrated the workouts with a very powerful calendar that will make it very easy to understand for trainees.

Theme Highlights

Responsive & Retina Ready

This theme is optimized for a Responsive viewing experience.

Front-End

HTML5 Animated Slider

The theme comes with only 1 slider and to be honest it’s all you will need. It is perfectly integrated with the the rest of the theme and it comes with some smooth out of the box HTML 5 animations.

Change colors with ease

Modify the highlight color with a single click. You can do that directly from the Wordpress admin, by using a simple color picker, and all instances of that color will be changed across the site.

Built in Workout Calendar

Keep your users up-to-date with all upcoming training sessions by adding your workouts into the built it workout module. Set recurring sessions, rest days and details for every exercise in the program.

Fitness Info

Every exercise and training session contains the body parts worked and the workout goals in this way your users will be able to easily pick their preferred workouts. You can also add videos and images for every exercise.

Change Fonts With Ease

Use a bunch of fonts from Google’s free collection, all accessible within our theme. Because the fonts are stored on Google’s servers, the displaying on your website will always be fast and reliable.

Built in Page Layouts

Besides the regular default blog pages, In Shape comes with a bunch of built in page layouts for you to use. From Workouts and Stories to Services and Pricing.

Sticky Navigation Menu

The theme comes with two types of navigation menu. The first option is a regular navigation that you can see on all our themes and the second a more modern one where the menu bar stays with you no matter where you scroll down the page.

Mega Menu

MegaMenu is a user-friendly, highly customizable responsive drop down menu. Our implementation makes it easy to create highly customized and creative mega menu configurations.

Back-End

Framework

The Fuse Framework is the backbone of our themes, with lots of settings and features that control the look and feel of the entire theme. See how it works by testing it on !

Shortcodes

A shortcode is a WordPress-specific code that lets you do nifty things with very little effort. Shortcodes can create objects that would normally require lots of complicated code in just one line.

Unlimited Sidebars

A useful module when, for example, you want to add more than one sidebar to a page, or different sidebars on different pages. This brings yet another layer of customization freedom to your website.

Translation Ready

All our WordPress themes are translation ready, so they support free or premium plugins, like qTranslate and WPML. Using these tools you can translate everything: pages, posts, menus, taxonomy, etc.

Powerful Contact Form Module

The theme comes with a contact form module that will enable you to create any contact form you might want or need. All this only a couple of clicks away.

Advanced SEO Options, Built-in!

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is vital for any website that wants to have as many visitors as possible, so we have built-in a great SEO module that offers you complete control & takes care of everything!
