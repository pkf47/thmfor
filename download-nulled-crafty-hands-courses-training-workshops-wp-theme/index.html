Download nulled Crafty Hands–Courses, Training, Workshops WP Theme


“Crafty Hands” is a WordPress theme suitable for arts, crafts, music, applied arts schools, courses and workshops. Comes with list of courses, teacher’s profiles, simple mini shop, event listing, calendar, blog, gallery + bbPress forums.

Download theme

Includes page templates (home, contact, shop, forum, courses, events, teachers, calendar) and 4 custom post types (teachers, courses, shop, events). Comes with multiple custom widgets and metaboxes.

No shortcodes, no page builders, no Bootstrap, no third party plugins (bbPress is optional, just in case you will want to integrate forums). You may read more on “About” section of live demo.

Logo, graphics, main full-width image are customisable via WP Customiser.

Tested on Chrome, Chrome Canary, Firefox, Safari, Opera, IE, Galaxy tablet.

Fits desktop screen resolutions and scales down to 1024px.

Looks fairly well on tablets (1280px):

Photographs are not included (stock photos, Wikimedia Commons), graphics used in decoration of the theme are included (with PSD for easy editing).

Credits: Google fonts: Poiret One, Muli
Social icons (included): GraphicsFuel
Scripts: ascensor.js, minicart.js (PayPal), fullcalendar.js (Google calendar)
