Download nulled Gridstone: Elegantly Built for Blogger


Gridstone, a content-focused blogging theme with easy vertical design and great typography that allows your visitor to enjoy the flow of reading across articles. The design is created to immediately create a sense of confidence to your visitor. Not too crowded with content, but just enough.

Gridstone allows to easily repositioning the sidebars (left, right or none), re-adjust the blog width and customize the color scheme through Theme Customizer. It also features flexible-touch-enabled carousel slider, integrated lightbox and post formats — just various ways to highlight your content.

Download theme

Primary Features
Theme Customizer to help you easily create your-only-skin. Fancy optional homepage sliders: - Carousel Flexslider Default Slider Three predefined layouts:- Right sidebar Left sidebar No sidebar. Responsive layout that adapts to the screen size of whatever devices your visitor are using. Layout width control to help you re-adjust your blog width. Optional related post feature that helps your visitor easily navigate across related articles. Optional random latest post. Just an easy way to highlight your latest content. Various post formats that perfectly blend with content Custom widgets:- Flickr Twitter Contact Images Author profile Well designed WordPress native widgets Content first orientation, great typography and white space with minimalistic design approach. Well designed archive page template Translation ready with RTL support jQuery lightbox integration Social share and network buttons
