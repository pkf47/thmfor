Due to our plan , we have just added additional language translation to Appic theme. For all Brazilian and Portuguese customers their native language is added. Appic is also available with French translation.

Download theme

Appic is a flat, clean and multi-purpose Wordpress theme built with responsive Bootstrap. It can be used for your corporate website or for any other sort of applications.

Appic has a responsive layout and a unique modern design. The layout will adapt to different screen sizes which will make your website compatible with any devices such as smartphones, tablets, laptop or desktop computers.

Appic has a lot of features suitable for a technology or a corporate website. In Wordpress admin panel, there is an easy way to add your services, your clients’ testimonials, portfolio, and even members of your great team! There are also Contact Form 7 and Mailchimp plugins integrated, so you can use them without worrying about the right styling. In addition, the most popular slider named Revolution Slider is included into theme. Appic theme is also fully compatible with the most popular plugin for translations named WMPL.

In Appic unique timeline you can indicate important historic dates of your company or let your visitors know about upcoming events. Blog page comes with Masonry which will show your articles in a random and intriguing view!

Appic theme is fully editable and can be easily customized to your needs. It offers over 600 Google fonts so no doubt you can create a special look for your website!

We hope you make a choice for Appic!
