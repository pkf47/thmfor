About Preface

Preface is a theme for Authors. Actual books, ebooks, manuals, stories – whatever kind of author you are, Preface is for you.

Download theme

You can now setup your own shop with full WooCommerce compatibility, complete with custom Preface styling!

Unique Touches

NEW! WooCommerce support added, via our Preface Extended child theme! Book Post Type with categorisation Build your Homepage quickly with our simple Homepage Items Book Launch Landing template Book Filter template Showcase your books from your 404 Page Clean and minimalistic blog (post formats supported: standard, gallery, image, link, quote, video, audio) Already own our Literary Theme? Preface’s Book post type is fully backwards compatible, so once you’ve bought this theme all of the books you had in Literary will appear in Preface! (You will need to upload your book covers at a higher resolution for Preface)

Color Options

You can control colours for the entire theme using the beautifully simple WordPress Theme Customizer. Advanced Font Options

You can add any font from Google Web Fonts, Typekit or Adobe Web fonts using our custom controls in the WordPress Theme Customizer. You’ll need a Typekit account and also a subscription dependent on font usage. You can even control all of the font sizes through the Theme Customizer.
