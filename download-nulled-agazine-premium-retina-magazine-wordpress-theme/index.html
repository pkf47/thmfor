Download nulled Agazine - Premium Retina Magazine WordPress Theme


Agazine is a blog and magazine style retina ready WordPress theme. Theme is widget ready and really easy to use. It can be used for online magazines, news sites or blogs.

Download theme

Agazine is also compatible with WooCommerce and bbPress. It is not only compatible but shop and forum are customized to match theme’s styling.

With responsive design and retina ready elements, it looks perfect on any kind of device.

Customer Review Features Widget Ready Homepage:
Homepage of this theme is widget ready. You can drag and drop various widgets/blocks to build a homepage of your own. Different Category/Blog Styles:
You can choose different layouts for categories. Every category can have different style from other categories. Responsive and Retina Ready Layout:
Layout of this theme is 100% responsive. This means that the theme will adjust it’s layout automatically according to screen resolution of device and content will be easily readable on any device like mobile phone, tablet, laptop or desktop PC. Custom Colors and Background for Every Category:
Every category can have it’s own color scheme and background image. You can make every category page different from other category pages by using different color scheme and background image. Unlimited Colors:
Different users have different color preferences. Lineza comes with unlimited color options so you can choose the color of your choice. Translation Ready:
Lineza is ready to be translated into any language of your choice. Theme comes with .po and .mo files and you can easily translate it to other languages. 5 Different Layouts:
You can choose from 5 different layout options. You can also choose for wide or boxed layout.
